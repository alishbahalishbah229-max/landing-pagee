About NIDS

What is NIDS?

Network Intrusion Detection System (NIDS) performs deep packet inspection & real-time traffic analysis to detect cyber threats instantly.

Why important?

Early breach detection, compliance (PCI-DSS, HIPAA), zero-day prevention and automated incident response across hybrid environments.

Key Features

Next-level network visibility with real-time intelligence

Real-Time Monitoring

Live traffic dissection & session reconstruction with millisecond latency.

Threat Detection

Signature & heuristic analysis, plus ML based behavioral modeling.

Alerts Generation

Customizable alerts via SIEM, Slack, PagerDuty, or webhooks.

Reports & Analytics

Automated executive summaries & forensic evidence packages.
